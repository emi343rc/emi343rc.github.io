Initial Narrative

This project was developed collaboratively as part of a group assignment. While some portions of the code were written by my teammates, I took the lead on designing the overall structure of the application and developing one of the three games included. I took great pride in the sections I implemented, striving to go beyond the project’s baseline requirements and deliver clean, organized, and functional code.

For future enhancement, my plan focuses on rebuilding or refining the two remaining games originally created by my teammates. My goal would be to polish their design, ensure smooth gameplay, and achieve a consistent user experience across all games. Although I may replace the Minesweeper game with a new one that better fits the project’s tone and mechanics, this enhancement plan would significantly improve the application’s quality, playability, and cohesion.
